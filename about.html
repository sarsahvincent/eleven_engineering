About

Eleven Engineering Ltd is a reliable and professional company, providing excellent quality and innovative solutions, in a cost effective, timely matter. We have been providing welding, machining, mechanical and fabrication services to Ghana and International customers. Our primary focus is in the oil, gas and construction sectors, although Eleven Engineering Ltd has experience in prototyping, General Engineering Services, Petroleum Pipeline Servicing, Welding and Fabrication, Training and Development, Engineering Consultancy Services, hydraulics, commercial construction, and underground services.
